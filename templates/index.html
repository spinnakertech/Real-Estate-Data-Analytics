Welcome to Real Estate Data Analytics


Real Estate Investors, Realtors, and Homeowners all benefit from insights into real estate markets. The links below explore three inquiries that will allow these groups to make informed decisions about when to purchase, sell, and advise clients about residential real estate markets in the United States.

The first inquiry, Impact of COVID-19 on the Real Estate Markets in 2020, looks at the impact of a market disrupter, in this case, the COVID-19 pandemic, on real estate markets.

The second inquiry, Predicting Real Estate Markets for Real Estate Investors, explores several economic factors and builds a Multiple Linear Regression machine learning model to predict future real estate markets.

The final inquiry, New Homes Sales--a Predictor of Existing Home Sales Real Estate Markets?, assesses whether New Home Sales are a reliable predictor of the Existing Home Sales real estate market.
